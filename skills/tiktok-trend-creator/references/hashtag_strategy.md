## 1. Hashtag Structure

Use a layered hashtag strategy (15-20 hashtags total):

**Tier 1 - Trend-specific (create 1-2 unique hashtags for the trend)**
- Format: #[SongName]Challenge, #[SongName]Trend, #[ConceptKeyword]
- These become the trend's identity. Keep them short, memorable, and easy to spell.

**Tier 2 - Niche hashtags (5-7)**
- Couples: #CoupleGoals #RelationshipHumor #CoupleChallenge #BfGf #CoupleComedy #ParejasGraciosas #Novios
- Humor: #FunnyTikTok #ComedyTikTok #HumorLatino #Viral #Risa

**Tier 3 - Broad reach (3-5)**
- #FYP #ForYouPage #ParaTi #Trending #ViralVideo

**Tier 4 - Music/artist (2-3)**
- #[ArtistName] #[SongName] #NewMusic

## 2. Caption Formulas

Effective caption patterns that drive engagement:

- **Call to action**: "Haz esto con tu pareja y etiquétame 😂" / "Do this with your partner and tag me"
- **Challenge**: "¿Tu pareja pasaría esta prueba?" / "Would your partner pass this test?"
- **Relatable**: "Dime que no soy el único 💀" / "Tell me I'm not the only one"
- **Curiosity**: "Espera al final... 😭" / "Wait for the end..."
- **Tag prompt**: "Etiqueta a alguien que haría esto" / "Tag someone who would do this"

Always include both Spanish and English versions when targeting Latin American + global audiences.

## 3. Publishing Tips

- Post during peak hours: 7-9 PM local time for Latin America, 12-2 PM and 7-9 PM for US
- First 48 hours are critical for algorithm pickup
- Respond to every comment in the first hour
- Create 2-3 variations of the same trend concept to test which performs best
- Use TikTok's native text and effects when possible (algorithm favors native tools)
